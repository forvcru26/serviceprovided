EL SEGUNDO, Calif.–(BUSINESS WIRE)–Mattel, Inc. (NASDAQ: MAT) announced today a new collection from across the portfolio of Mattel brands inspired by the highly anticipated film, Barbie\u2122. Created in partnership with Warner Bros. Pictures, the upcoming movie, Barbie\u2122, debuts in theaters nationwide July 21st. Fans can now take home a part of the movie and play out even more stories with toys from brands including UNO\u00ae, Hot Wheels\u00ae, Fisher-Price\u00ae, MEGA\u2122 and Barbie.<\/p>\n

The line of Barbie\u2122 the Movie dolls features looks fans will see in the movie, reflecting the signature styles of Barbie, Ken, and new characters as they traverse through Barbie Land and beyond. The collection emulates the Barbiecore\u2122 and Ken-ergy\u2122 felt in the feature film, from their first-look outfits to the matching sets worn in the movie. Best of all \u2013 the collection features several recognizable and must-have accessories, including a mini replica of the iconic three-story DreamHouse\u2122 from MEGA and a stunning pink RC Corvette\u00ae from Hot Wheels.<\/p>\n

\u201cBarbie the movie is a monumental moment for the brand, and we cannot wait for fans to experience Barbie like never before on the big screen,\u201d said Lisa McKnight, Executive Vice President and Global Head of Barbie & Dolls, Mattel. \u201cThe new line of Barbie the movie Mattel toys perfectly captures the fun and joy of Barbie that is at the heart of the film. This collection allows fans of all ages to celebrate their favorite characters and scenes from the movie. What an incredible way to kick off the summer of Barbie!\u201d<\/p>\n

Barbie\u2122 The Movie doll line includes:<\/p>\n

    \n
  • Barbie The Movie Doll Pink Gingham Dress: Models a vintage-inspired pink and white gingham dress, complete with daisy chain necklace and pastel pink heart-adorned pumps.<\/li>\n
  • \nBarbie The Movie Ken Doll Pastel Stripe Matching Set: Wears a beachy, pink and mint green striped matching set with surfboard in tow.<\/li>\n
  • \nBarbie The Movie Doll Plaid Matching Set: Touts an impeccably coordinated three-piece matching pastel plaid set with puffy skirt, cropped jacket, and matching blouse.<\/li>\n
  • \nBarbie The Movie Ken Doll Denim Matching Set: Clad in an all-Demin look with an open vest and matching light wash jeans, featuring a glimpse of iconic Ken boxers.<\/li>\n
  • \nBarbie The Movie Doll Gold Disco Jumpsuit: Shines in a sequined disco jumpsuit featuring big disco hair, metallic jewelry and golden heels.<\/li>\n
  • \nBarbie The Movie Doll Pink Western Outfit: Wears an eye-catching, Western-themed matching pink denim vest and flare jeans with a cowboy hat and boots.<\/li>\n
  • \nBarbie The Movie Doll Gloria Pink Power Pantsuit: Looks fierce in a three-piece pink suit featuring satiny blazer, matching blouse, and pinstriped trousers with fashionably split cuffs.<\/li>\n
  • \nBarbie The Movie Ken Doll Gold and White Disco Tracksuit: Embodies luxe quality and casual style in a white tracksuit with metallic gold color-blocked sleeves and a shiny \u201cK\u201d accent to add star power to the ensemble.<\/li>\n
  • \nBarbie The Movie Doll President in Pink and Gold Dress: Wears a full, satiny gown that features a sparkly off-shoulder bodice and shimmery gold accents that trail down her skirt.<\/li>\n
  • \nBarbie The Movie Fashion Pack: Includes three iconic Barbie outfits from the movie: a blue and white sundress, gingham pink blouse and short set, and dreamy pajama outfit.<\/li>\n
  • \nBarbie The Movie Pink Corvette Convertible: Featuring a curvy retro silhouette and pink paint job, with film-inspired details like white wall tires and chrome rims.<\/li>\n
  • \nAlong with Barbie items inspired by the characters in the movie, Barbie\u2122, Mattel is also releasing a collection of products celebrating the live action feature film across the larger Mattel portfolio, including:<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n

    UNO Barbie The Movie: Deck features graphics inspired by characters and scenes from Barbie The Movie \u2013 including a special \u201cPlayed With Too Much\u201d rule that will have players both discarding and drawing cards.
    \nLittle People Collector Barbie The Movie: Bringing four of the film\u2019s iconic characters down to Little People figure size and styled in memorable outfits including Barbie, President Barbie, Ken, and Gloria.
    \nBarbie The Movie MEGA Dreamhouse: Buildable replica of Barbie\u2019s iconic 3-story DreamHouse as seen in the film, including spiral staircase, pool, and slide.
    \nBarbie The Movie Hot Wheels RC Barbie Corvette: Radio-controlled 1956 Corvette Stingray that can fit two Barbie dolls to create authentic road trips with Barbie.
    \nBarbie The Movie Hot Wheels Die-Cast Pink Corvette in 1:64 Scale: Features Barbie deco \u2013 in Barbie pink \u2013 as a Hot Wheels 1:64 scale die-cast car.
    \nThe Mattel collection of the movie Barbie\u2122 products, available in collectible packaging, will be at retailers nationwide and\/or Mattel Shop for an SRP of $1.25 to $150 starting June 1st.<\/p>\n

    In continued celebration of the film, Mattel will also be releasing an array of movie-themed products with over 100 brand partners across fashion, beauty, accessories and more starting this month.<\/p>\n
